Schwamm Drauf!

Schwamm Drauf! is a data story about the climate in the city of Bamberg. It shows the importancce of fine-grained measurement in inner cities (with different climate zones) and the varying temperatures in dependence of urban structures.

baRADies?!

Another data story about cycling in and around Bamberg. Using data from Stadtradeln 2024 the story offers various perspectives on how the traffic infrastructure is used by cyclists. Furthermore the users can personalize the story by taking a short survey, and explore the data on interactive visualizations
